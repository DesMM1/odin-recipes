Grilled Salmon

Description

Salmon is one of our all time favourites and we think this recipe will make it yours! Check it out below.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets: 1 ½ pounds
  • Garlic powder: Add to taste
  • Salt: Add to taste
  • Soy sauce: ⅓ cup
  • Brown sugar: ⅓ cup
  • Water: ⅓ cup
  • Vegetable oil: ¼ cup

Steps

  1. Season your salmon fillets: Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Stir, bag, refrigerate: Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat grill: Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Cooking time: Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
