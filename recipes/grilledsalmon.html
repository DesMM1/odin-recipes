Grilled Salmon

Description

Salmon is one of our all time favourites, we think this recipe will make it yours! Check out the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Stew meat: Two pounds of beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch pieces.
  • Flour: All-purpose flour will thicken the broth, creating a hearty stew.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and a bay leaf.
  • Broth: Store-bought or homemade beef broth.
  • Vegetables: Carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery.

Steps

  1. Combine your ingredients: Place the beef in the slow cooker, then toss with flour, salt, and pepper. Add the broth, vegetables, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
  2. Set slow cooker: Cover the Crock-Pot and cook on Low for 8 to 12 hours or on High for 4 to 6 hours.
