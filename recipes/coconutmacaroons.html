Coconut Macaroons

Description

Attention all coconut lovers! These little bundles of joy are a great finisher to one of our recipes, or just as a late night snack. Here's our take on Coconut Macaroons.

Ingredients

  • Coconut: 5 Â½ cups of flaked coconut
  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the cookies structure
  • Salt: Salt is known to enhance flavour, just a pinch will do
  • Sweetened condensed milk: A 14-ounce can (key ingredient)
  • Vanilla: Add to taste

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients: Stir the dry ingredients in a bowl, then mix in the wet ingredients.
  2. Scoop n' drop: Use a small ice cream or cookie scoop to drop the mixture onto prepared cookie sheets.
  3. Cooking time: Bake in the preheated oven until the coconut is lightly toasted.
